# Manzil Musharakah vs Murabaha vs Typical Canadian Bank Mortgage

| Dimension | Manzil Halal Musharakah | Murabaha (e.g. Manzil / Islamic banks) | Typical Canadian Bank Mortgage |
|-----------|-------------------------|----------------------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **Philosophy** | Joint ownership (shirkah): lender and borrower are partners who co-own the asset; borrower gradually buys out lender’s share. Risk and reward shared on the asset; no interest (riba). | Cost-plus sale: lender buys the asset and sells it to the borrower at a disclosed cost plus profit, paid in installments. No interest; profit is from a sale, not from lending money. | Debt and interest: bank lends money; borrower owes principal plus interest (riba). Lender does not own the asset; borrower bears asset risk; interest accrues over time and can compound. |
| **Legal form** | Partnership (co-ownership) with diminishing lender share. | Sale contract (buy then resell at cost + profit). | Loan (debt) secured by a charge/mortgage on the property. |
| **Who owns the property at start** | Lender and borrower jointly. | Lender (until sold to borrower). | Borrower (subject to bank’s charge). |
| **Lender’s return** | From sale of its share (e.g. at agreed price) as borrower buys it out. | Agreed profit margin on the sale price (fixed or tiered). | Interest on the loan (fixed or variable). |
| **Risk on the asset** | Shared (partnership). | Largely with borrower after sale (lender held asset briefly). | Borrower (bank has collateral, not ownership). |
| **Typical rate / pricing (Canada, indicative)** | Variable, e.g. BoC + margin (~4.45% cited). 5-year fixed product. | Fixed profit rate, e.g. 3.49%–5.49% (Manzil). | 5-year fixed ~3.69%–5.14% (market); big banks ~4.79%. |
| **Minimum down payment (Canada)** | Often 20% (e.g. Manzil). | Often 20% (e.g. Manzil). | 5% on first $500K + 10% above that; 20% if no insurance. $1M+ often 20%. |
| **Amortization / term** | 5-year fixed term; buyout over agreed period. | Often 1–25 years (e.g. Manzil). | Term 1–5+ years; amortization up to 25–30 years. |
| **Prepayment / early exit** | Structure-dependent; often flexible. | No prepayment penalty (e.g. Manzil); may have rebates. | Depends on contract; penalties common on fixed rates. |
| **Shariah compliance** | Yes (partnership, no riba). | Yes (sale with disclosed profit, no riba). | No (interest-based). |
| **Total buyer eventually pays** | Down payment + buyout of lender’s share. At same rate as conventional, total is similar (e.g. \$400K at 4.45% over 25 yr → down + ~\$664K buyout ≈ \$764K total). | Down payment + sale price (cost + agreed profit). Total fixed at outset (e.g. \$400K financed → \$926K payable over 25 yr → \$1,026K total with \$100K down). | Down payment + principal + interest. E.g. \$400K at 4.79% over 25 yr → down + ~\$687K in payments ≈ \$787K total. |

## Example house purchases (CAD): 500K, 750K, 1M

| Item | Manzil Musharakah | Murabaha (Manzil-style) | Typical Canadian Bank Mortgage |
|------|-------------------|--------------------------|--------------------------------|
| **$500,000 house** | | | |
| Min. down payment | 20% = $100,000 | 20% = $100,000 | 5% = $25,000 (insured) or 20% = $100,000 |
| Amount financed / partnership | $400,000 | ~$400,000 (sale price = cost + profit) | $475,000 (if 5% down) or $400,000 (if 20% down) |
| Rate type (illustrative) | Variable ~4.45% | Fixed e.g. 4% | 5-year fixed e.g. 4.79% |
| Approx. monthly payment (25-yr, 20% down) | ~\$2,212 (on \$400K at 4.45%) | ~\$3,087 (Manzil example; total payable \$926K on \$400K over 25 yr) | ~\$2,290 (on \$400K at 4.79%) |
| **Total buyer eventually pays** (20% down, 25-yr) | ~\$764,000 (down \$100K + ~\$664K buyout at 4.45%) | ~\$1,026,000 (down \$100K + \$926K sale price per Manzil example) | ~\$787,000 (down \$100K + ~\$687K P&amp;I at 4.79%) |
| **$750,000 house** | | | |
| Min. down payment | 20% = $150,000 | 20% = $150,000 | 5% on first $500K + 10% on $250K = $50,000 (insured) or 20% = $150,000 |
| Amount financed / partnership | $600,000 | ~$600,000 | $700,000 (if 5%+10% down) or $600,000 (if 20% down) |
| Rate type (illustrative) | Variable ~4.45% | Fixed e.g. 4% | 5-year fixed e.g. 4.79% |

| **$1,000,000 house** | | | |
| Min. down payment | 20% = $200,000 | 20% = $200,000 | 20% = $200,000 (no insurance above $1M in many cases) |
